AutoKing Offers

Car Maintenance: We understand that regular car maintenance is important for keeping your vehicle in top shape, and that's why we offer a wide range of services to help you achieve this. From oil changes to filter replacements, tire rotations to brake inspections, our team of experienced technicians will ensure that your car is running smoothly and efficiently.

Mechanical Repairs: We know that unexpected mechanical issues can be stressful and frustrating, but you can trust our team to get your vehicle back on the road quickly and safely. We offer a comprehensive range of mechanical repairs, from fixing small leaks to replacing major components of your engine. Whatever the issue, we've got the expertise to diagnose and repair it.

Servicing: Your car deserves the best care possible, and that's exactly what we provide with our comprehensive servicing. Our team will give your car a full check-up, including all components, fluids, and systems, to identify any potential issues that may need attention. We'll make sure your car is running smoothly and safely, so you can enjoy the ride with confidence.

Safety Certificate Inspections: If you're driving a light vehicle, trailer, or a booked hire vehicle such as UBER or didi, you need to ensure that it meets the necessary safety standards required by law. Our Approved Inspection Station (AIS) can provide you with Safety Certificate inspections (roadworthy / RWC / COI) to ensure that your vehicle is safe to drive on Australian roads. Our experienced technicians will inspect your vehicle's brakes, steering, suspension, tires, and lights to make sure it meets the legal safety standards.
